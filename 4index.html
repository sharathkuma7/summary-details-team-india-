            Bumrah is the fastest Indian pacer to take 150 wickets in Test cricket and the second fastest 
            Indian bowler to reach 100 wickets in One Day International (ODI) cricket. 
            He is the first bowler to have achieved the number one ranking in 
            the ICC men's player rankings in all three formats of the game.

Bumrah career path

             Bumrah was signed by the Mumbai Indians (MI) for the 2013 season of the IPL. Since then, he has played
             for MI, contributing to the team's title wins five times. He is the second-highest 
             wicket-taker for MI, behind Lasith Malinga, with 165 wickets in 133 matches.
