Rohit Sharma

Born

            Sharma is an aggressive batsman but plays with style and elegance.
            He is usually an opening batsman in limited overs cricket,
            but has played most of his Test cricket as a middle-order batsman.            
            In limited overs cricket, Sharma is widely recognised as one of the format's most 
            outstanding batsmen.

Rohit Sharma achievements

        Sharma holds several batting records which famously include most sixes
        in international cricket, most double centuries in ODI cricket (3), most centuries at Cricket World Cups (7)
        and joint most hundreds in Twenty20 Internationals (5). Rohit Sharma is the first player to score 5 T20I centuries.
