ALL MS DHONI

Born

M.S. Dhoni (born July 7, 1981, Ranchi, Bihar [now Jharkhand] state, India) is an Indian cricketer
    whose rise to prominence in the early 21st century culminated in his captaincy of the Indian
     national team that won the one-day 
    Cricket World Cup in 2011. Dhoni made his international debut in 2004.

Ms dhoni career path

            Dhoni made his international debut in 2004. 
            His talent with the bat came to the fore in an innings of 148 runs against 
            Pakistan in his fifth international match. Within a year he joined the India Test team,
            where he quickly established himself with a century (100 or more runs in a single innings) 
            against Pakistan.
